How to Use WaterWise Tools

Follow these steps to ensure accurate water quality data collection and entry.

📍 Location & 📅 Date

Location: Enter the name of the site (e.g., River Nile, Borehole A3).

Date: Use the date picker in DD/MM/YYYY format.

📊 Measuring Parameters

  • 🧪 pH Meter: Calibrate with standard buffer solutions (pH 4.00, 7.00, 10.00). Rinse probe with distilled water before/after use. Insert probe into water sample. Wait for the reading to stabilize. Record the value, clean, and store probe in storage solution.
  • 🌫 Turbidity Meter (NTU): Use clean cuvette with no air bubbles or fingerprints. Fill with sample, insert into meter. Wait for stable reading. Record NTU value (high NTU = cloudy water).
  • 🌡 Thermometer (°C): Submerge digital thermometer in the sample. Wait 30–60 seconds for temperature to stabilize. Record the temperature.
  • ⚡ Conductivity Meter (µS/cm): Calibrate with standard solution. Rinse and insert probe into sample. Wait for a stable reading. Record electrical conductivity (indicates ion concentration).
  • 💧 Dissolved Oxygen Meter (mg/L): Calibrate the DO meter. Place probe in water sample. Wait for a steady reading. Record the oxygen level (important for aquatic life).
  • 🌊 Salinity (ppt): Often measured via multi-parameter meter. Place probe in sample. Record salinity reading directly or calculate using conductivity.
  • 🧪 TDS Meter (mg/L): Insert probe into water. Wait for a stable reading. If calculated: TDS ≈ EC × 0.65. Record total dissolved solids value.
  • 🏗 Hardness Test (mg/L CaCO₃): Use a hardness test kit. Follow titration instructions until color changes. Calculate and record the value.
  • 🧪 Alkalinity Test (mg/L CaCO₃): Use a titration kit. Add reagent drop by drop. Stop when color changes, calculate using provided chart. Record alkalinity level.
  • 🧴 Chlorine Test (mg/L): Use test strip or color comparator. Dip in sample and compare color with chart. Record free/total chlorine levels.
  • 🦠 Total Coliforms / E. coli (CFU/100 mL): Use a membrane filtration or presence/absence test. Incubate sample, count colonies (CFU). Record count or presence.

🌊 Water Source

Select the appropriate water source: River, Lake, Borehole, Tap, Rainwater, etc.

📝 Additional Notes

Add context: e.g., weather conditions, recent rainfall, visible pollution, flow conditions, etc.

✅ Submitting the Form

Ensure all fields are filled. Click "Submit" and wait for confirmation.

🛠 Troubleshooting

  • Always calibrate equipment before use.
  • Use clean containers and avoid contamination.
  • Double-check units (mg/L, CFU, NTU, etc.).
  • If unsure, refer to equipment manuals or consult a supervisor.
